Burrito

A simple, fast and tasty lunch!

This burrito is quick to cook and assemble. Tasty, filling and fast lunch for your day!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Large Tortillas
  • Beans
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Yellow Onion
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Dice the onion
  2. Cook and drain the beef
  3. Add the onion
  4. Stir in the beans
  5. Add cheese and melt it
  6. Fill and fold the tortillas
  7. Butter a new pan
  8. Cook burrito to seal the seam
  9. Let them cool and enjoy!
