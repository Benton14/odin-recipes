Asian Chicken

Asian ground chicken is a wonderfully tasty dish that is very versitle and can be eaten many ways. Wrapping it up in letuce is always a good option but enjoying the dish with a side of rice is a great meal as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 Large carrot (grated)
  • 1/2 Red bell pepper
  • 1 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 Lb Ground chicken
  • 4 Green onions

Sauce

  • Cloves garlic (minced)
  • Inch piece of ginger (grated)
  • Tsp lime juice
  • 1/4 Cup soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • Tsp cornstarch

Directions

  • Step 1: Wash, grate, chop carrot and pepper, then mix together and set aside.
  • Step 2: Mince and grate the garlic and ginger.
  • Step 3: Mix together all sauce ingredients and set aside.
  • Step 4: Add oil to medium skillet. Add chicken to skillet and brown.
  • Step 5: Once chicken is completley browned, add sauce to chicken in skillet. Bring to boil and cook 1-2 minutes, until thickened. Turn off heat.
  • Step 6: Stir in green onions, carrots, and red bell pepper.
  • Step 7: Serve hot and enjoy!
