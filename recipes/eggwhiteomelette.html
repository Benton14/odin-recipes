Egg White Omelette

A simple, easy and healthy breakfast!

This egg white omelette is fluffy, delicious and loaded with veggies. It comes together quickly and is a healthy way to start your day!

Ingredients

  • Liquid Egg Whites
  • Baby Spinach
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Yellow Onion
  • Parmesan
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Prep the veggies
  2. Heat the pan
  3. Add oil
  4. Saut√© the veggies
  5. Pour the eggs over the veggies
  6. Add the parmesan
