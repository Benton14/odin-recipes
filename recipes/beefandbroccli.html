Beef & Broccoli

A tasty dinner sure to satisfy all

A fairly fast & tasty dinner to please even the pickiest of eaters!

Ingredients

  • Flank Steak
  • Broccoli
  • Cornstarch
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Sesame Oil & Seeds
  • Soy Sauce
  • Brown Sugar
  • Water
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Add rice & water to a cooker and start it
  2. Combine steak & cornstarch in a bag
  3. Shake to caot
  4. Add oil to a pan & cook steak
  5. While cooking steak, mix garlic, sesame oil, soy sauce, water, and brown sugar
  6. Pour over steak
  7. Reduce heat to medium
  8. Add broccoli
  9. Cook until broccoli is tender but crisp
  10. If sauce is thin add more cornstarch
  11. Plate rice
  12. Add the beef and broccoli overtop
  13. Sprinkle seeds for aesthetics
  14. Serve and enjoy!
