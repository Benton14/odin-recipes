Tres Leches Cake

The best cake hands down!

Most delicious cake that was graceously given to humans by God himslef.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milks
  • Heavy Cream
  • Cinnamon
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Whip egg whites and sugar into stiff peaks & set aside
  2. Beat together butter and sugar until creamy
  3. Stir in egg yolks and vanilla extract
  4. Gently fold in egg whites
  5. Pour into a cake pan
  6. Bake for 35-40 minutes
  7. Let it cool
  8. Poke holes throughout the cake
  9. Drizzle three milk mixture over the cake
  10. Cover & chill overnight
  11. Frost with whipped cream
  12. sprinkle finely ground cinnamon over the cake
  13. Cut, serve and enjoy!
